Product details


The Yamaha F310P Acoustic Guitar is designed to help beginners and casual guitarists hone their musical skills. This guitar features a spruce top that emphasises the high notes and makes them clearer. The nato neck is joined with a rosewood fretboard to produce a soothing warm tone with excellent sustain. This guitar has a scale length of 634mm that gives more clarity and sharpness to the sound. The vibration caused in the strings are transferred to the guitar top accurately via a rosewood bridge. This ensures guitarists hear the correct tone they desire. The open chrome tuners help the guitarist set the precise tension for each string.
